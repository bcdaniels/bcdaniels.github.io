Collective Cognition

September 2014

tags: Video

In the summer of 2014, I helped organize a working group at the Santa Fe Institute to explore the nascent field of collective cognition. We had fun chatting with thinkers from diverse fields, and this video summarizes some resulting thoughts and speculations about what collective cognition is all about and where it's going.
